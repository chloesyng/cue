# cue

**four people. one night. every choice changes the plot.**

most social apps are built to keep you online: swipe more, scroll more, open another group chat, then somehow figure out how to meet in real life.

cue does the opposite.

cue is an interactive product prototype for an AI-directed group dating experience. instead of matching you with one person and hoping the conversation survives, cue brings four people into one night and quietly produces what happens next.

it chooses the group, plans the date, changes the pairings, sends private missions, reveals anonymous interest, and steps in whenever the energy needs a push.

no public group chat. no endless texting. no pressure to decide who you like before actually meeting them.

the experience changes in real time based on who chooses whom.

## what makes cue different

most dating apps stop at the match.

cue starts there.

it does not just introduce people—it directs the experience after they meet. throughout the night, participants receive private prompts, pairing switches, side-conversation invitations, anonymous interest reveals, and small real-world missions designed to create moments that probably would not happen naturally.

think less “dating app.”

more “an invisible producer running your night.”

## the experience

cue guides participants through the full journey:

- group matching and compatibility
- availability confirmation
- a real-world date plan
- rotating pairings
- private choices and anonymous signals
- side conversations
- missions and golden tickets
- a final interest selection

every participant sees the experience from their own private point of view, and their decisions can change what the rest of the group experiences next.

## why i built it

i kept wondering why dating apps put so much effort into getting people to match, then leave the hardest part completely up to them.

what if the product did not disappear once people met?

what if it could remove the awkwardness, create momentum, and quietly shape a night worth remembering?

so i built cue.

## about this prototype

cue is an independent portfolio concept designed and built by Chloe Ng.

this repository contains a self-contained front-end prototype with fictional participants, simulated messaging, multiple user perspectives, and demo controls. it is a product exploration—not a launched dating service.
